**中文说明 / Chinese Description:**
此列表展示了日本动画旅游协会在2020年评选的"日本必访动漫圣地88"中北海道和东北地方的内容。每个条目都列出了对应动漫作品的取景地或原型所在的真实地区。

**English Description:**
This list showcases the Hokkaido and Tohoku regions from the 2020 edition of "88 Must-Visit Anime Holy Sites in Japan" selected by the Japan Animation Tourism Association. Each entry highlights the real-world locations that served as filming sites or inspirations for the featured anime works.
